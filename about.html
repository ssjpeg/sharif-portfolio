SADIA SHARIF
ABOUT

Sadia Sharif is a junior studying computer science at Columbia Univerisity. She is an illustrator, and is currently looking to find ways to intersect tech with design.

Born and raised in Queens, NY.

In 2021, she began doing research under CS Professor Lydia B. Chilton in understanding the ideation process behind creating news focused illustrations. She has also participated in research on AI generated art, and finding a purpose for the tool.

In her free time, she is designing shirts, experimenting with 3D printing, and lion dancing.

Skilled in Java, C, HTML/CSS/JavaScript, Python, Photoshop, After Effects, and has a working knowledge of Future 360 and Blender.
