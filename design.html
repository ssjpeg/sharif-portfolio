FRUITFUL

This is an app created by Sadia Sharif and Janice Li to encourage people to be more sustainable.

The app surveys the user on their habits to create a personalized list of goals for them to complete.

Users are given the opportunity to connect with friends, engage in friendly competition, and gain insight on local community events.

BARTENDING ACADEMY

This is an app developed by Sadia Sharif, Justin Saintil, and Jason Zou

Bartending Academy teaches someone the basics of bartending and mixology through a lesson plan and quiz

Each developer contributed to developing the frontend, backend, database, and assisted in debugging for all aspects of the site.
